# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection
This application does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## How We Use Spotify Data
- Authentication is handled entirely by Spotify's OAuth system
- We access your Spotify data only to generate playlists based on your preferences
- No user information is saved on our servers
- No database or server-side storage is used

## Session Management
- Users must re-authenticate each session
- No cookies or persistent sessions are maintained
- All data is processed client-side only
